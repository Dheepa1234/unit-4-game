Crystals Collector !

Click here for instructions.

The computer will choose a random number to start the game.


There are four crystals, the value of each is hidden until you click on it. By clicking on a crystal you will add points to your score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the computer.


You lose the game if your total score goes above the computer.


Each time when the game start, the game will change the values of each crystal.
